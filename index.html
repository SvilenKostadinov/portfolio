    Some facts about Michael Jordan

  • Jordan was born February 17, 1963 in Brooklyn, New York
  • He played three seasons at the University of North Carolina 1982-1984
  • The Chicago Bulls selected Jordan with the third overall pick in 1984 NBA draft
  • NBA Rookie of the Year – 1984 1985
  • Six-time NBA champion – 1991, 1992, 1993, 1996, 1997, 1998
  • Six-time NBA Finals MVP – 1991, 1992, 1993, 1996, 1997, 1998
  • Five-time NBA MVP – 1988, 1991, 1992, 1996, 1998
  • 10-time NBA scoring leader – 1987–1993, 1996–1998
  • Three-time NBA All-Star Game MVP – 1988, 1996, 1998
  • Named one of the 50 Greatest Players in NBA History in 1996
  • FIBA Hall of Fame – class of 2015
  • Two-time Olympic Gold Medal winner
